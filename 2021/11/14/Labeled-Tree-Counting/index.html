$T(z) = \sum_{k\ge 0} T_k \frac{z^k}{k!}$

$T’(z) = \sum_{k\ge 1} T_{k} \frac{z^{k-1}}{(k-1)!}$

$\int T(z) = \sum_{k\ge 0} T_{k} \frac{z^{k+1}}{(k+1)!}$

$\frac{1}{z} T(z) = \sum_{k\ge 1} \frac{T_k}{k} \frac{z^{k-1}}{(k-1)!}$

$z T(z) = \sum_{k\ge 0} (k+1)T_k \frac{z^{k+1}}{(k+1)!}$
